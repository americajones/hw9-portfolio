About Me


LinkedIn Profile | GitHub Profile

I am America Jones and I am a student of the world. You can call me AJ.

I've been an illustrator for the past ten years and am now starting my professional journey into programming. I've always been interested in both tech and art, and I am excited to combine my two passions.

Most of all I love creating something from nothing. To my brain, coding feels like a puzzle game I don't ever want to put down.

I like to spend my free time loving my wife, walking my dog, and studying various languages. I'm a quick learner who likes to pick up all sorts of things.

My major strengths are thinking outside the box, keeping it calm and cool under pressure, and being very difficult to discourage.
